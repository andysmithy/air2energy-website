01 Decarbonising what can't be electrified

Turning pollution
into power.

Fossil fuels power the modern world, and that's not changing any time soon. But with how damaging emissions are, the pressure to act is real — and it needs to be now.

Air2Energy is here to help, using retrofitting technology to keep businesses running while providing them with a sustainable energy source.

Through our retrofit system, we provide a solution that isn't 10 years away. By retrofitting an electrochemical system onto existing gas boilers, we filter CO₂ from the exhaust and convert it into electricity — turning pollution into power.

03 The technology

At Air2Energy, we are making pollution an asset.

Our technology and chemical process is what makes our solution innovative. Our solution involves filtering CO₂ instead of letting it escape and pollute the atmosphere, then converting that waste into energy for the building to utilise.

01 · FILTER

By attaching the system to the exhaust of the gas boiler, CO₂ is filtered with little to no operational changes throughout the whole process.

02 · CONVERT

An electrochemical reaction converts the captured CO₂ into usable electricity.

03 · POWER

The building is then given clean electricity to use however they want, ultimately reducing energy costs and Scope 1 emissions.

04 Where it works

Are you a building that runs on gas? Then we are perfect for you.

Building owners and commercial property groups are our primary market, with NABERS-rated buildings still using gas as our current priority.
